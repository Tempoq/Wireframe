2 Edna Street Goodna


Traditional Queenslander with a Modern Twist... Plus 7026m2 Block!!!


This beautiful home has the perfect mix of tradition and modern comfort.


Set on 7026m2 of level accessible land, 2 Edna Street is the perfect escape...

without leaving behind the conveniences of living close to everything.


You can let your heart run wild with enough space for horses, gardens, a hobby

farm... or space for every vehicle, boat, caravan, trucks - whatever you can think of.


Set amongst low maintenance gardens, this home site offers multiple garages and

sheds as well as a beautiful home featuring three bedrooms, renovated bathroom,

fireplace and a modern kitchen featuring views of the huge entertainment deck and

the rest of the property.


Under the home you will find ample storage space as well as a very generous

laundry.


Open for inspection on Sunday and by private appointment. Contact Tait at Coronis

today to discuss this fantastic property.
